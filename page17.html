Beltei International School

Beltei International School, Takhmao City in Cambodia, is the fouteen branch , is set up to train a lot of student each year with Vision and Mission as below.

Their VISION is a peaceful, developed Cambodia, where:

  • Poor youth receive a quality education,
  • Moral and spiritual values are upheld,
  • All generations play an active role in the decision-making and nation-building process..

Their MISSION is:

  • To inspire hope,
  • To nurture life, and
  • To promote human dignity
